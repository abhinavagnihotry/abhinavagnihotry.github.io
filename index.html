What's the website about?

This website is a personal project of mine to showcase my knowledge and skills in web development. The webiste is divided into three different parts. The ABOUT-ME page is the profile and works as my resume in a very interactive form. BLOG sections is the place where you and I can share our ideas. The Blog page is designed using soft colors for easy reading.
